Empowering Tomorrow's Entrepreneurs, Makers, and Community Leaders

Program Focus

Entrepreneurship

AlphaLab Gear and Innovation Works bring startup and entrepreneurial expertise to our prgram, covering things like the Lean Startup and the Business Model Canvass, while exposing program participants to successful entrepreneurs.

Makership

TechShop brings Makership knowledge to the program, exposing participants to dozens of pieces of advanced manufacturing equipment. Everything from the wood shop and machine shop, to laser cutters, 3d printers, CNC machines and more. Additionally, they train participants in software tools like AutoDesk Fusion 360 and the Adobe Creative Suite.

Community Engagement

G Tech connects our participants to a strong network of local leaders and community stakeholders, which help our participants identify needs in their community and gives them a framework to solve problems they encounter.

Program Info

Learning

412Build is a program for students 16-19 that offers valuable experience in entrepreneurship, makership, community development, and play. Each year 24 students are selected from a large and highly competitive pool of applicants to participate in 412Build's summer program. Over the course of each full-time, five-week session, students will learn from experts through a series of curated workshops and lessons including:

3D printing

Entrepreneur Panels

Financial Planning

Prototyping

Market Research

Urban Design

Welding

Woodworking

Doing

Participants then apply these new skills and knowledge by turning vacant land in an East End neighborhood into a valuable community asset. After conducting market research to understand the preferences of the community, the participants design, prototype, build, and install products of their own creation on the lot sites. Participants leave the program not only with payment for their hard work, but with a valuable set of skills and experiences that will continue to serve them as they continue on to college, the workforce, or start companies of their own.
