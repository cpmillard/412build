Empowering Tomorrow's Entrepreneurs, Makers, and Community Leaders

Entrepreneurship

We ignite the fire of entrepreneurship in our 412 Builders. First we construct an entrepreneurial framework and mindset through a number of workshops, lessons and hands-on activities. We cover things like the Lean Startup and the Business Model Canvass, in addition to bringing in local entrepreneurs and CEOâ€™s to share their experiences. Then, we step back and let our Builders take the lead and give them the power to make the decisions that they think will lead them to success.

Makership

We train our 412 Builders on dozens of pieces of advanced manufacturing equipment. Everything from the wood shop and machine shop, to laser cutters, 3d printers, CNC machines and more. By the end of the program our Builders have used almost every piece of equipment in TechShop and have been trained in software tools like AutoDesk Fusion 360 and the Adobe Creative Suite. Our builders leave the program with the foundational skills needed to fill the 600,000+ unfilled advanced manufacturing jobs in the United States, as well as the ability and drive to fuel the revolution of independent makers in the U.S.

Community Engagement

We help our 412 Builders give back to the community by using their newfound skills to address the problems they see their community facing every day. By reaching out to the community before they start their projects, our Builders assess the most pressing needs in their community and tailor their projects to address these issues. Our Builders create partnerships with community stakeholders and learn how to make a lasting social impact through their work.
